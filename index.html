About Me

Hi, my name is Scott, and I am a consummate procrastinator.

My life mantra seems to be: "The best part about procrastination is that you are never bored, because you have all kinds of things that you should be doing." Also, I literally declare a "to-do" list bankruptcy every month or so, just so that I can start over and keep the list from growing to more than 100 line items.

Aside from extreme procrastination, my hobbies include: skiing, reading, video games, tennis, coding, cooking, baking, wine tasting, and swimming.
